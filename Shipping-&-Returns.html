Shipping & Returns

Last Updated: October 18, 2024

Shipping Policy

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped. Delays may occur during peak sales periods.

2. Domestic Shipping Rates and Estimates (Nigeria)

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

  • Lagos Metropolitan Area: Flat rate of NGN 3,500. Delivery in 1–2 business days.
  • Major Cities (Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano): Flat rate of NGN 5,000. Delivery in 3–5 business days.
  • Rest of Nigeria: Calculated at checkout based on weight/location. Delivery in 5–7 business days.
  • Free Shipping: Available on all orders over NGN 100,000.

3. International Shipping

We offer shipping to select international destinations. Shipping rates and delivery times will be calculated at checkout. Please note, international orders may be subject to import duties and taxes (including VAT) which are incurred once a shipment reaches your destination country. Lara's Collection is **not** responsible for these charges.

Returns & Exchanges

1. Return Window

We accept returns and exchanges for unworn, unwashed, undamaged items with all original tags attached within 14 days of delivery.

2. Eligibility & Non-returnable Items

  • Items must be returned in their original packaging.
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse will not be accepted.
  • **Non-Returnable Items:** For hygiene reasons, all **Jewelry**, **Traditional Perfumes/Attar**, and **Sale/Clearance** items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

3. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log in to your account via the My Account page.
  2. Navigate to "My Orders" and select the order you wish to return.
  3. Click the "Request Return" button and follow the steps provided.
  4. Once approved, you will be provided with a return shipping label and instructions.

If you have any further questions, please contact us at support@larascollection.com.
