Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping times depend on your location:

  • Lagos: 1–2 business days.
  • Major Cities: 3–5 business days.
  • Other Areas: 5–7 business days.

Note that processing time (1–2 business days) is separate from shipping time.

For full details, visit our Shipping & Returns page.

We offer a **14-day return window** from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags. Note that **jewelry and sale items are final sale**.

To start a return, please log in to your account and go to the "My Orders" section.

Our detailed size chart is available on every **Product Detail Page (PDP)**. We recommend checking the specific measurements for each product, as fits can vary slightly between collections.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and common Nigerian payment gateways like Paystack and Flutterwave.
